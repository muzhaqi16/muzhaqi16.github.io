About Me

I've been passionate about programming for as long as I can remember. About two years ago, I quit my job and decided to enroll in Thinkful's coding Bootcamp to pursue my passion as a web developer.

Since then, I haven't stopped exploring and learning what to build with code. The ability and the power that the code gives me make me want always to learn more.

Aside from coding, I also love to travel, explore the world, meet new people, and try to plan solutions to real-world problems with code. I have gone to over 20 countries so far and built about as many projects.

My Qualities

Having the Skillset

I am always learning new technologies because I know that it's important to branch out so that when the time comes, and I have to work with a technology that I am not familiar with, I'll at least know what's involved and not be intimidated by it.

Driven to Continue Learning

I am always looking to expand my knowledge consistently to keep up with the industry. Being a good developer means that you aren't afraid to learn a language that you aren't familiar with and monitor how technologies and languages change to stay current.

Problem Solving Mindset

Even though my brain doesn't think in code yet, keeping a broad understanding of how the whole code works, gives me a lot more insight into how each little part connects to implement the entire application.

Excellent communication skills

As a web developer, it can be really easy to go silent, but communication with your team is essential. I always take time to communicate what I'm working on and report any obstacles that I might run into.
